America's Finest City

Most Popular Activities
to Do in San Diego, CA

Balboa Park

"If you had to visit only one place in San Diego,
this is it. Most of the museums and the zoo
are located here. Great place to stroll around.
Dedicate a full day to this place if you want to
do it at a leisurely pace."

Windansea Beach

"Calm water, soft sand, and easy access.
Abundant free parking. Clean beach to relax
as well as for kids to play."

Gaslamp Quarter

"It's walking distance to all the fine restaurants,
hotels, bars, clubs, musuem, convention center,
bay, seaport village, EVERYTHING."

Mission Beach

"The birds and sea creatures are so accustomed
to people that I was able to get right up to them,
it was a unique experience. The beach is clean
and loaded with various shells if you enjoy collecting
them. The other people were friendly and everyone
seemed to be having a great time!"

Sunset Cliffs

"It's free and the views are breathtaking. They
have tide pools and many areas of
beach to explore. A lot of surfers."
